“Now when he came nigh to the gate of the city, behold there was a dead man carried out, the only son of his mother, and she was a widow: and much people of the city was with her. “And when the Lord saw her, he had compassion on her, and said, Weep not. “And he came and touched the bier: and they that bare him stood still. And he said, Young man, I say unto thee, arise. “And he that was dead sat up, and began to speak. And he delivered him to his mother. “And there came a fear on all. And they glorified God, saying, That a great prophet is risen up among us; and That God hath visited his people.”
