We visited the Dancing Dervishes. There were twenty-one of them. They wore a long, light-colored loose robe that hung to their heels. Each in his turn went up to the priest (they were all within a large circular railing and bowed profoundly and then went spinning away deliriously and took his appointed place in the circle, and continued to spin.
